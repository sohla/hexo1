audio software development | interactive sound design

Steph O’Hara is an independent Audio Software Developer and Sound Designer.

With over twenty years experience in both development and designing all things with sound,
Steph has worked across the globe, engineering and producing sound and software.
Steph continues to use machines to make sounds every day.
Sometimes he gets paid by other people to do it.
Other times he is working on his own commercial and personal projects.
He also does it any other time, regardless of what else he is doing.

He loves it.

Steph currently specialises and offers development services in

  • Interactive Sound Design (Theatre, Installations)
  • Apple Audio development (iOS, tvOS, macOS)
  • Web-Audio Development
  • Studio Audio Mixing and Engineering
